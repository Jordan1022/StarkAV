## Services

### AV Design And Consulting

Stark AV designs and advises on audio, video, and production systems for churches. Engagements may include assessment, system design, recommendations, documentation, budgeting guidance, and technical consultation.

### Install Support

Stark AV can be as involved in the install process as the church wants. That may include helping select an installer, coordinating with an integration partner, reviewing installation decisions, or handling more of the process on behalf of the church.

When a project needs skills beyond Stark AV's direct scope, the company can bring in subcontractors or complementary specialists. This should be positioned as a strength: churches get a clear guide and the right people around the table without having to manage every technical relationship themselves.

### Church Production Team Training

Training for production volunteers should help teams understand their systems, serve with consistency, and feel more confident in their roles during services.

### Worship Team Training

Training can support worship teams as they work with production systems, stage sound, monitoring, service flow, and healthy collaboration with production volunteers.

### Worship Leadership Team Training

Training can support worship leaders and ministry leaders with the practical decisions that shape Sunday services, team communication, production expectations, and long-term system stewardship.

### Light Corporate And Government Consulting

Stark AV can lightly support corporate and government AV needs, especially conference rooms and meeting halls. This should remain secondary to the church-focused positioning.
